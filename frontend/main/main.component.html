Your First Step to Fitness




“Insanity: Doing the same thing over and over again and expecting different results.” ~Albert Einstein

If you’re like most people, you’ve been on a diet which failed, then you try another diet and so on. This cycle of failure, misinterpretation, and repeated attempts constitutes what Polivy and Herman have called the “false hope syndrome.” Successful self-change requires escaping this cycle and moving toward a more realistic and sustainable path.



The journey of transformation begins here







“A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.” ~Lao Tzu

Everyone who ever lost weight or “got in shape” started on a single day. That day turned into a week, a month, and eventually a year. It didn’t happen overnight and it wasn’t always easy. But, persistence pays off when it comes to health. And, starting with a single step today can pay off and bring you closer to the healthy and fit body you’ve always hoped for.










You may have to fight a battle more than once to win it.” ~Margaret Thatcher

Changing your eating and activity habits is not always easy. It is important to realize that if your last attempt to lose these habits has failed, it doesn’t mean you should give up for good! Free yourself of the fads and the typical approaches to weight loss and fitness that are unlikely to work—and don’t despair!
